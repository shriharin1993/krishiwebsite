Hydroponics

Hydroponics farm in north Bangalore. Ideation for vast organic and mechanized farming underway

Agriculture carried out using state-of-the-art technology. The combo of technology and agriculture makes it the perfect pair to serve the agri needs of today.

Hydroponics farming enables in maintaining a clean surrounding and reduces the maintenance of soil to grow plants. With dissolved mineral solvents into water, this technology enables easy growth and quick results.

The nutrients used in the solvent include byproduct from fish waste, duck manure, or purchased chemical fertilisers. But they are not limited to these alone and obtain their manure from various other sources.

Techniques

Static solution culture

In static solution culture, plants are grown in containers of nutrient solution, such as glass Mason jars (typically, in-home applications).

Continuous-flow solution culture

In continuous-flow solution culture, the nutrient solution constantly flows past the roots.


Aeroponics

Aeroponics is a system wherein roots are continuously or discontinuously kept in an environment saturated with fine drops (a mist or aerosol) of nutrient solution.

Fogponics

Fogponics is a derivation of aeroponics wherein the nutrient solution is aerosolized by a diaphragm vibrating at ultrasonic frequencies.
