Control your data and your social experiences

Because you publish directly on the blockchain, your content and related-actions are always publicly verifiable. You retain custody of the content you create and the platform can never delete what you publish. Also, on Murmur, content curation lies in the hands of the user, not some algorithm. Be a part of the blockchain-enabled future

Using Murmur requires you to have an EOS blockchain account. To activate your account, you must stake EOS tokens. Every action associated with this account, including those that you take on Murmur, is captured permanently and directly on the EOS blockchain and remains publicly verifiable. This ensures platform transparency like no other.




Wandx

WandX is the world’s first multi blockchain decentralized token exchange with a hedging product powered by the Basket Protocol.

  • Multi blockchain desktop app

    The WandX desktop app is a decentralized token trading platform on Ethereum and NEO blockchains for ERC20 and NEP5 tokens respectively. To support Wanchain and Aion shortly.

  • Hybrid decentralized exchange ecosystem

    Trade tokens issued on blockchains like Ethereum, NEO, and Wanchain while always holding control of your tokens. Orders are hosted on the blockchains and trades happen directly through them.

  • Ethereum and NEO wallet

    Take advantage of the WandX app to connect or create Ethereum, NEO, Wanchain and Aion wallets to sign transactions securely and fast, ensuring complete control over your wallets at all times.
